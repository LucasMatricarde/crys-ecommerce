**StrainTag** — pill that classifies a product's strain (indica/sativa/hybrid) and shows its THC reading. Use on product cards and listings.

```jsx
<StrainTag type="indica" thc="THC 28%" />
<StrainTag type="sativa" thc="THC 24%" />
<StrainTag type="hybrid" thc="THC 22%" />
```

The dot + label adopt the strain accent — violet (indica), amber (sativa), green (hybrid). The THC value renders in Space Mono.
