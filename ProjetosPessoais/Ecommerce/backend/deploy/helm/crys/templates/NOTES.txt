Startup ordering
----------------
Kubernetes has no docker-compose `depends_on`. Services may start before postgres /
mongo / kafka are ready and will restart until they connect — readiness probes gate
Service traffic and Spring connection retries + container restart converge the stack
once infra is Ready. This is expected; watch them settle:

  kubectl get pods -w

Access
------
  # api-gateway (only external entrypoint) — NodePort {{ (index .Values.services "api-gateway").nodePort }}, or:
  kubectl port-forward svc/api-gateway 8080:8080

  # observability
  kubectl port-forward svc/jaeger 16686:16686
  kubectl port-forward svc/prometheus 9090:9090
  kubectl port-forward svc/grafana 3000:3000
